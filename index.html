About Me

I am Napholeon Cerdeña, an artist and creative based in the Philippines. As an artist, my primary goal is to express and explore ideas rooted in my personal experiences, the people I meet, and the places I visit. For me, staying true to oneself and to the people and surroundings is essential to discovering one's unique voice and identity as an artist. This journey is not an overnight process but rather a spontaneous and evolving one.
On the other hand, as a creative, my objective shifts from self-expression to collaboration. I aim to work alongside other professionals—whether they are artists, creatives like myself, or from entirely different fields. The focus is on showcasing my artistic skills and creative ideas to achieve a shared vision and desired outcome
